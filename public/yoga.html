Yoga Trainings

Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga is a foundational style that focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama). It helps improve flexibility, balance, and overall well-being.

Benefits: Increased flexibility, stress relief, improved posture.

Vinyasa Flow

Vinyasa flow is a dynamic style that synchronizes breath with movement. It provides a cardiovascular workout, enhances strength, and promotes mental focus.

Benefits: Cardiovascular fitness, muscle toning, mental clarity.

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini yoga aims to awaken the dormant energy within the body. It involves dynamic movements, breathwork, and meditation to achieve spiritual awareness.

Benefits: Spiritual awakening, increased energy, stress reduction.
